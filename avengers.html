THE AVENGERS

When the mischievous god Loki arrives on Earth wielding the powerful Tesseract, a mysterious energy source, he threatens global security. In response, Nick Fury, director of S.H.I.E.L.D., initiates the Avengers Initiative — a plan to assemble a team of extraordinary individuals. Reluctantly, Earth's mightiest heroes — Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, The Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye — must learn to work together despite their clashing egos and differences. As Loki opens a portal to unleash an alien army on New York City, the newly formed Avengers must unite to defend Earth from destruction.
